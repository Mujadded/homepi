✨ WHAT'S NEW:

1. 🔁 REPEAT MODE EXPLAINED
   ────────────────────────
   ❌ Repeat OFF (default) = Play song ONCE
   ✅ Repeat ON = Loop song FOREVER until stopped
   
   💡 For normal schedules: Leave repeat OFF
   💡 For alarms: Turn repeat ON if you want it to keep playing

2. ⏱️ TRACK DURATIONS
   ────────────────────
   ✅ Duration shown for every song (e.g., "3:24")
   ✅ Duration shown in schedules
   ✅ Now you can spot schedule conflicts!
   
   Example:
   07:00 - Morning Song (3:24) → ends 07:03
   07:05 - Next Song (2:15) → starts 07:05
   ✅ No conflict!

3. 📊 LIVE PROGRESS BAR
   ────────────────────
   ✅ See current position in song
   ✅ See total duration
   ✅ Visual progress bar
   ✅ Updates every 2 seconds
   
   Display: "1:32 / 3:24" with progress bar

4. 🔊 PLAYBACK VOLUME CONTROL
   ────────────────────────────
   ✅ Adjust volume while song is playing
   ✅ Independent from default volume
   ✅ Real-time control

5. 🎛️ CLEARER BUTTONS
